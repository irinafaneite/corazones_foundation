About the Event

We invite you to join us for the most prestigious evening of our calendar year. The Corazones of Courage Gala is more than just a celebration; it is a catalyst for change. For over a decade, we have gathered the most passionate leaders and advocates to support our mission of providing mental health resources and educational scholarships to underprivileged youth.

This year, we are proud to announce our keynote speaker, Dr. Elena Rodriguez, who will share her groundbreaking research on community-led healing initiatives. The evening will feature a curated five-course dinner, a silent auction featuring works from local artists, and live musical performances by the Metropolitan String Quartet.

Every ticket purchased directly funds our 'Future Leaders' program, which has already helped over 500 students achieve their dreams of higher education. Your presence makes a tangible difference in the lives of those who need it most.
